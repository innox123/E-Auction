Multi Factor Authentications.

MFA adds an extra layer of security beyond passwords alone. Even if an attacker manages to obtain a user's password, they would still need access to the additional factor(s) to gain entry. This significantly reduces the risk of unauthorized access.

Account takeover attacks, where unauthorized individuals gain access to user accounts, become more prevalent without MFA. ATO attacks can lead to data breaches, financial losses, identity theft, and reputational damage for individuals and organizations.
